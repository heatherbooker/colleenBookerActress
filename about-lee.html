About Lee

Actress


Lee Booker is an actress best known for Up and Down (2014), X-Acto (2012) and The Super Alliance (2013). Lee is involved in film, television, commercials, voice acting, and shorts. She has been nominated for a Joey award. She is currently based out of Vancouver, Canada.
